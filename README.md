## 🛠 Troubleshooting

- **App won’t install:** Check if you allowed installation from unknown sources.  
- **Bluetooth or NFC won’t work:** Make sure Bluetooth and NFC are turned on in your device settings.  
- **Cannot find parking location:** Try moving the phone closer to the car’s NFC tag or ensure Bluetooth is active.  
- **App crashes on startup:** Restart your device and try again. Update your Android system if possible.  
- **Server connection issues:** Verify your server settings or contact your server administrator.
